Secure Vault & Payment Methods

These two modules are intentionally not shown here. Both hold real client secrets (portal logins, bank account/routing numbers) that are now encrypted server-side — but this demo page is a broad, unauthenticated-feeling convenience tool, and even metadata browsing across every client isn't the right thing to expose from it.

What exists and is fully working (verified directly against the real database, not just this page): Secure Vault — create/list/reveal/delete a client's encrypted secrets, admin-only, every access logged — at /vault/:clientId. Payment Methods — encrypted account/routing numbers, last-4 shown for display only — at /payment-methods/:clientId.
