# Tasks + Rules — Legacy vs New App

## Summary
Tasks: ~30 legacy items reviewed — roughly 10 Match, 9 Partial, 11 Missing. Task Detail: ~24 legacy items reviewed — roughly 8 Match, 7 Partial, 9 Missing. Rules + Batches: ~19 legacy items reviewed — roughly 4 Match, 4 Partial, 10 Missing (plus 1 New "Recent Batches" history table not present in the legacy UI).
