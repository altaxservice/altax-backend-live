## Summary of methodology
For every function, the relevant new-app backend route file(s) were read in full (not grepped in isolation) and cross-checked against `sql/schema_source.json` for missing tables, plus a sample of `frontend/src/pages/*.tsx` (TasksListPage, InvoicesListPage, ClientDetailPage areas) to confirm no UI-only capability was missed. A repo-wide grep (`nodemailer|sendgrid|smtp|twilio|sendEmail|mailgun`) confirmed the new app has **no actual email/SMS sending infrastructure anywhere** — every legacy function that calls `GmailApp.sendEmail` or formats an SMS payload is therefore capped at PARTIAL at best (a communications *log* may exist) or MISSING (no log mechanism at all for that scenario either).
