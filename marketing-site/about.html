About Us

Empowering Maryland's small businesses since 2020

Established in 2020, AL TAX SERVICE is a full-service Tax, Accounting, and Corporate Services firm dedicated to empowering individuals, entrepreneurs, and small business owners throughout the Maryland region. We specialize in providing reliable, personalized financial and business solutions that help our clients navigate the complexities of the U.S. tax system and maintain compliance with ever-changing federal, state, and local regulations.

Our mission is to simplify tax and financial management, allowing small-business owners to focus on what they do best — growing their businesses. From tax preparation and strategic tax planning to bookkeeping, payroll, business formation, and corporate compliance, we offer comprehensive services tailored to meet the unique needs of each client.
