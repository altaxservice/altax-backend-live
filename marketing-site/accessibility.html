Accessibility Statement

Our commitment to making this website usable for everyone, including people with disabilities.

Last updated: July 18, 2026

Our Commitment

AL TAX SERVICE is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people of all abilities. We are actively working to increase the accessibility and usability of this website and, in doing so, to adhere to relevant accessibility standards.

Conformance Standard

We aim to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA. These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible for people with disabilities, and more user-friendly for everyone.

Measures We Take

  • Semantic HTML structure and landmarks to support screen readers
  • Text alternatives for images and icons
  • Keyboard-navigable menus, forms, and interactive controls
  • Sufficient color contrast between text and background
  • A responsive layout that adapts to screen size and zoom level

Known Limitations

Despite our best efforts, some parts of this website may not yet be fully accessible. We are aware of this and are working to address it. If you encounter a barrier, please let us know using the contact information below — we want to hear from you.

Feedback

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of this website. If you experience any difficulty accessing any part of this site, please contact us at +1 (443) 835-4492 or visit us at 1714 Saint Paul St. STE 1A, Baltimore, MD 21202. We try to respond to accessibility feedback within a reasonable timeframe.
