SMS & WhatsApp Communications Terms

Details of our text messaging and WhatsApp program, including how to opt in, message frequency, and how to opt out at any time.

Last updated: July 18, 2026

Program Description

The AL TAX SERVICE SMS & WhatsApp Program sends text and WhatsApp messages related to your relationship with our firm, including appointment confirmations and reminders, tax deadline reminders, requests for documents needed to complete your filing, and general updates about services you've requested. We do not send unsolicited marketing text messages.

How to Opt In

You may opt in by checking the SMS/WhatsApp consent box on our Contact page, or by verbally providing your mobile number and consent to a member of our team during a consultation or onboarding.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on your engagement with our services. You can generally expect between 1 and 4 messages per month, with more frequent messages around tax filing deadlines.

Message & Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply, depending on your mobile carrier and plan.

How to Opt Out

You may opt out of SMS/WhatsApp messages at any time by replying STOP to any message you receive from us, or by contacting us directly at +1 (443) 835-4492. After you opt out, we will send one final message confirming your removal from the program.

Help

Reply HELP to any message for assistance, or call our office directly at +1 (443) 835-4492.

Carrier Liability

Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

Your Privacy

Your mobile number and opt-in consent will never be sold, shared, or rented to third parties for marketing purposes. See our full Privacy Policy for details.
