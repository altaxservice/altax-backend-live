Privacy Policy

How AL TAX SERVICE collects, uses, and protects your information — including our SMS and WhatsApp communications program.

Last updated: July 18, 2026

Overview

AL TAX SERVICE ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect through this website, how we use it, and the choices you have — including your choices around SMS and WhatsApp messages.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Contact form submissions (name, company name, phone number, email address, and the details of your inquiry)
  • Newsletter sign-up (email address)
  • Mobile phone number and consent status, if you opt in to receive SMS or WhatsApp messages from us
  • Your language preference, stored locally in your browser

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and schedule consultations
  • Provide the tax, accounting, payroll, and corporate compliance services you request
  • Send appointment reminders, tax deadline reminders, and service-related updates by phone, email, SMS, or WhatsApp — only where you've consented
  • Improve our website and services

SMS & WhatsApp Communications

If you opt in to receive text messages or WhatsApp messages from AL TAX SERVICE, your mobile phone number and consent will never be sold, shared, or rented to any third party or affiliate for marketing or promotional purposes. Opt-in data collected for SMS/WhatsApp messaging is used solely to deliver the communications you signed up for. Message and data rates may apply, and you may opt out at any time by replying STOP. Full program details are available in our SMS & WhatsApp Terms.

How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with trusted service providers who help us deliver our services (such as e-filing and payroll processing platforms), and only to the extent necessary to perform those services. We may also disclose information if required by law.

Cookies & Local Storage

This website uses your browser's local storage to remember your language preference (English/Arabic). We do not currently use tracking or advertising cookies.

Data Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect the information you share with us. No method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Choices

  • Opt out of SMS/WhatsApp messages at any time by replying STOP, or by contacting us directly
  • Unsubscribe from our email newsletter using the link in any newsletter email
  • Contact us to ask what information we hold about you, or to request it be corrected or deleted

Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for individuals 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above reflects the most recent revision.
